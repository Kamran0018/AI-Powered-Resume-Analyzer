Expert Resume Tips
Do's
  • Use standard section headings
  • Include keywords from job descriptions
  • Quantify your achievements with numbers
  • Keep resume to 1-2 pages
  • Use bullet points for readability
  • Include a professional summary
  • List technical skills prominently
Don'ts
  • Avoid using tables or columns
  • Don't use fancy fonts or graphics
  • Avoid generic phrases like "hardworking"
  • Don't include irrelevant personal info
  • Avoid spelling and grammar errors
  • Don't use PDF with images only
  • Avoid overused buzzwords
